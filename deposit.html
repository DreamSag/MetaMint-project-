Deposit Kaise Karein?

1. Neeche diye gaye USDT TRC20 address ko copy karein.
2. Apne Crypto Wallet se is address par rakam bhejein.
3. Payment ke baad transaction ka Screenshot (Slip) le lein.
4. Screenshot upload karein aur Submit karein.

⚠️ Tawajjo: Address dehan se copy karein. Ghalat address par bheji gayi rakam zaya ho sakti hai.

💡 Agar aapko koi step samajh nahi aa raha, toh turant Customer Service Center se rabta karein.
